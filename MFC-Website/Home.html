ABOUT US

Welcome to the Markuss Fight Club (MFC), A UFC 5 competitive Discord league.

------MFC------

At MFC, we host weekly fight cards that mirror the excitement of real-world UFC events. Our matches are scheduled every week at 6 PM GMT, bringing together top players from around the world to compete in thrilling battles.

------HOW TO JOIN------

Joining is simple. Become a member of our Discord server and stay updated for sign-up announcements. When it's time, register through the designated channel and secure your spot on the card.

------Watch Live------

All our events are streamed live on Twitch, featuring high-quality broadcasts with commentary and analysis. Tune in to catch all the action and support your favorite fighters.

------More Than Just Fights------

Beyond regular matches, we offer money tournaments with cash prizes and exhibition fights for those looking to hone their skills. MFC is more than a league; it's a community of passionate fight enthusiasts. Join us on Discord and Twitch, and be part of the ultimate UFC 5 experience. Welcome to MFC, where the fight never ends!
